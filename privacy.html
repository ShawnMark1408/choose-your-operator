Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 March 2026

BetMatch (bet-match.us) is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how it is used. The short version: we collect very little, we don't sell anything, and your quiz answers never leave your browser.

Who We Are

BetMatch is a free, independent operator-matching tool based in the UK. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@bet-match.us.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. There is no sign-up form, no account, and no mechanism by which we receive your name, email address, or any other identifying information unless you contact us directly by email.

Quiz answers are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. They are not transmitted to any server and are not stored beyond your current session.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage (not traditional HTTP cookies) for two limited purposes:

  • Cookie consent: We store a single flag (bm_cookie_ok) to remember that you have acknowledged this notice, so we don't show it to you again.
  • Quiz state: Your in-progress quiz answers may be temporarily held in memory during your session. This data is not persisted after you close the tab.

You can clear localStorage at any time by clearing your browser's site data. This will reset the cookie consent banner on your next visit, but will not affect anything else.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookie-based analytics.

Google Fonts

This site loads fonts (Bebas Neue and Inter) from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests these fonts, your IP address is sent to Google's servers. This is standard practice for web font delivery. Google's privacy policy applies to this data: policies.google.com/privacy.

We have no control over and take no responsibility for how Google processes this data. If you are concerned, browser extensions that block Google Fonts will prevent this request.

Third-Party Links

BetMatch contains links to external sites — including BeGambleAware, GamStop, GamCare, and Gambling Therapy. These sites have their own privacy policies and we have no responsibility for their content or practices. Links to external sites open in a new tab.

We do not currently operate any affiliate links. If this changes, this policy will be updated and a clear disclosure will appear on any relevant page before any such link.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain or delete. The only data stored locally is the cookie consent flag in your browser's localStorage, which you can remove at any time by clearing your browser data.

If you contact us by email, we will retain your email address and the content of your message for as long as it is relevant to handle your enquiry or feedback. We will not share this information with third parties.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have rights including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Since we do not collect personal data through the website, these rights most commonly apply if you have contacted us by email.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@bet-match.us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

Questions about this policy or anything else? Email us at hello@bet-match.us.
